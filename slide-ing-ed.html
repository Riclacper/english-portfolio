Adjectives in English: ING & ED

How to use adjectives ending in ING and ED

ING

Adjectives ending in ING describe things, situations, or experiences.
The class is interesting.
The movie is boring.
The lesson is tiring.
The game is exciting.
❌ Wrong: I am boring.
✅ Correct: I am bored.

ED

Adjectives ending in ED describe how people feel.
I am interested in English.
I am bored.
I am tired after class.
I am excited about the test.
Example:
The movie is boring, but I am interested.
