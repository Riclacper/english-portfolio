Rec’n’Play Experience

Rec’n’Play is an event held in Recife that brings together technology, innovation, creativity, and education. The event offers lectures, workshops, and activities that connect students and professionals with current trends in the technology market.

My experience at Rec’n’Play was very important because I had contact with new ideas, current technologies, and professionals in the field. The event showed how technology is present in different areas and how learning goes beyond the classroom.

For the Analysis and Systems Development (ADS) course, Rec’n’Play is fundamental because it helps students better understand the job market, encourages the search for knowledge, and reinforces the importance of practice and constant updating.

Participating in this event increased my motivation in the course and contributed to expanding my view of professional possibilities in the technology field.
